Design Review

Zoya's Webpage that will be the focus of this review

EX-1 Font

The Design of the website is good and the choice of Fonts are also good but The lack of usage of CSS zen template kinda takes away from the experience of seeing font make a change in the design of a website

Other than that the overall design of the page is nice, could use a better set of colors though

EX-2 Color

The color palette was excellent, with consistent styling throughout.

The dark mode color choice was particularly well-done.

The HSL and gradients were also well-executed.

EX-3 Icons

The icons were subtle and fit well with the content and styling.

Until and unless I am misunderstanding there are no SVG is level 2 its just icon packs

EX-4 Images

The image information was good, but could benefit from more details about image properties.

The styling and layout were consistent throughout.

The Level 3 image exercise was simple and effective.

EX-5 Sample Website

The article layout was good, with nice visual styling.

Adding more content to the article would be beneficial in seeing how it looks with long form content.

EX-6 Redesign

The redesign efforts were excellent, with good image annotations and a modern interface.

The UI/UX comparison b/w amazon and flipkar was amazing and seeing the reazoning was cool too.

I could not find the link to the redesigning of the page, either its missing during the writing of this review or I didn't find it

Final verdict is 8/10 design
