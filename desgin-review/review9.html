Design Review

Shilpa's Webpage that will be the focus of this review

EX-1 Font

The font choices were good, especially in Level 1 where the CSS Zen Garden template was used.

Consistency with the template would be really appreciated because we can clearly see the progression of changes

EX-2 Color

The color palette was consistent and well-chosen, making the design look great.

The overall design of the page was good and easy to understand

EX-3 Icons

The icons could benefit from more context and explanation.

Outside of that the usage of Icons here shows they understand how to use icons

EX-4 Images

Adding more context and information about the images would be helpful.

Right now, the images are just presented without much explanation or context.

EX-5 Sample Website

The article website looks very good as it minimalist with no clutter and distracting elements

The 2nd website also looks very good, the usage of cards is very nice touch giving the user a small thumbnail and a small into to the content

EX-6 Design in Practice

The redesign efforts were good on the IRISET/IRCTC website, but over all strictly speaking the page still looks quite cluttered and little hint to where the user is supposed to go or what to do

The various reasoning behind the bad designs are very valid though

Final verdict is 8/10 design
