Design Review


Shifath's Webpage that will be the focus of this review

EX-1 Font

The font choices were excellent, with good spacing and typography.

The code had a well-chosen color scheme, and the background color complemented the font well.

The only issue was an inaccurate title in one section.

EX-2 Color

The color palette was consistent and easy on the eyes.

However, some areas could benefit from more information about the color choices.

In one case, the gradient on a button could be improved.

EX-3 Icons

The use of icons was seamless and well-integrated.

However, some icons could benefit from clearer explanations and better color choices.

In one case, the icon grid could be improved with clearer labels.

EX-4 Images

The use of images was good and the color choices complemented it.

The images provided a good context, and the responsiveness of the website was excellent.

The website also lazy-loaded images and provided appropriate alt text.

EX-5 Sample Website

The article layout was well-organized and easy to follow.

The information was well-presented, and easy to follow along

EX-6 Design in Practice

The redesign efforts demonstrated a good understanding of design principles.

The annotations provided were helpful in understanding the design choices.

However, some redesigns could benefit from better image choices and color schemes.

Final verdict is 8/10 design
