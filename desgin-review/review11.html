Design Review

Balaji's Webpage that will be the focus of this review

EX-1 Font

The font choices and styling were excellent throughout.

The transition from one level to the next was smooth and well-executed.

The only suggestion is to improve the structure of the information presented.

EX-2 Color

The color palette was good, but could benefit from more consistency.

The dark mode color choice was particularly well-done.

The color swatch generator was also well-executed.

EX-3 Icons

The icons were well-chosen and complemented the color scheme.

The icon implementation was good, but could benefit from clearer labels.

The download and upload buttons were well-designed.

EX-4 Images

The image implementation was good, with clear information about the changes made.

The image properties could benefit from more details.

The overall image exercise was well-done.

EX-5 Sample Website

The medium clone was excellent, with good spacing and margin guidelines.

EX-6 Redesign

The redesign efforts were good, with a modern interface and nice hover effects.

The UI/UX comparison could benefit from clearer winners and more annotated images.

Final verdict is 8/10 design
