Design Review

Varun Tharala's Webpage that will be the focus of this review

EX-1 Font

The font exercise was good, providing all necessary information and showing the reasoning and effect of certain decisions.

The background color was easy on the eyes

EX-2 Color

The color exercise was really cool, content was a bit lacking in my opinion to see the full extent of the color themes but still really nice.

The color choices were excellent, and the dark and light themes complemented each other well.

EX-3 Icons

The icon exercise was well-done, with good color choices and a well-implemented design.

Providing clearer labels and using better icons and proper aligining and sizing of icons in some places would be beneficial.

EX-4 Images

The image exercise was good, with nice designs and layouts.

Adding more context and information about image properties would be helpful.

EX-5 Sample Website

The layout exercise was well done, with a well-designed landing page that was consistent in styling.

usage of cards was really well done

The redesign efforts were outstanding, with excellent annotations and a clear flow of information.

The redesigns for the IRCTC website and Press release page were well-done, but could benefit from adding a date.

Final verdict is 8/10 design
